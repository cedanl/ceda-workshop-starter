Good questions. Here are honest answers.

We believe in transparency — ask us anything you don't see covered here.

Is Nebula open source?

The Nebula CLI and core runtime are MIT-licensed and available on GitHub. The hosted platform (cloud infrastructure, dashboard, and managed CI/CD) is a commercial service with a generous free tier.

How does Nebula handle database deployments?

Nebula integrates natively with Postgres, MySQL, and Redis. Schema migrations are run atomically as part of each deployment, and Nebula automatically creates a database snapshot before applying changes so you can roll back instantly.

Can I self-host Nebula?

Yes. The Enterprise plan includes a self-hosted distribution you can run in your own VPC or on-premise environment. It includes the full feature set plus dedicated support from our infrastructure team.

What frameworks does Nebula support?

Nebula supports any containerized application, with first-class presets for Next.js, Astro, Remix, Django, Rails, and Laravel. If your stack isn't listed, you can bring your own Dockerfile and Nebula will handle the rest.
