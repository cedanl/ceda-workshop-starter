Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Pacific Time. Pro and Enterprise customers receive priority routing and can expect a first response within 2 hours. Enterprise customers on the 24/7 add-on have access to round-the-clock phone and Slack support.

How do I report a security vulnerability?

We take security seriously. Please disclose vulnerabilities responsibly by emailing security@nebula.dev. We will acknowledge your report within 24 hours and aim to resolve confirmed issues within 72 hours. We maintain a public bug bounty program — details are available on our security page.

Can I request a live product demo?

Absolutely. Use the contact form above or email sales@nebula.dev to schedule a 30-minute demo with a solutions engineer. We tailor every demo to your specific use case and tech stack — no generic slide decks.
