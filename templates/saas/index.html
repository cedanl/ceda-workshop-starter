Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you get started.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

You get full access to all Pro features for 14 days with no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or downgrade to the Free plan. Your data is never deleted.

Can I migrate my existing data into Nebula?

Yes. We provide a guided import wizard that supports CSV, JSON, and direct integrations with Notion, Airtable, and Linear. Our onboarding team assists Enterprise customers with custom migrations at no extra cost.

Is Nebula GDPR compliant?

Absolutely. Nebula is GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2 Type II compliant. All data is stored in encrypted form, and you can request a full data export or deletion at any time from your account settings.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you at 80% and 100% of your limits. Your account won't be suspended — we give you a 30-day grace period to upgrade or adjust usage before any restrictions apply.
